Meet the Kensington Kennels team

We have three full-time staff to help give your dog a personalised service and make sure
they are happy, well exercised and kept healthy while you are on holiday.

Jane Smith

Manager

Jane is an experienced Kennel Manager who has comprehensive dog care experience as well as a strong love for animals. She is also a trained veterinary nurse.

David Black

Senior Kennel Assistant

David ensures that the dogs are well cared for - creating detailed reports to monitor dog conditions and making sure the animals have clean and sanitary living spaces.

Faye Winters

Kennel Assistant

Faye is responsible for grooming and exercising the dogs. She has a Pet Nutrition Diploma and ensures that each dog has an individually planned nutricious diet.
